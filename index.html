JINX FTRE

Hanoi-based producer & DnB enthusiast Jinx FTRE (also known as Colleen Hoang) is still making progress, not day by day but improving and proving music is her biggest passion. Having been a raver since 2011, Colleen's knowledge of dance music is the one that makes her production diverse and somewhat unique.

Signed on Universal Music Vietnam for her collaboration with Curdz, BINNE and My Linh "Stay With Me", Colleen is on her way to get things going the next years.
